Consultancy

Services

FRP-Coating

FRP-Coating prevents chemical tanks from corrosion and makes the tanks anisotropically stronger than steel.

Cooling tower spare parts supply

We also supply accessories of cooling tower like sprinkler, cooling tower fan, sprinkler pipe, cooling-coil,etc.

Descaling chemicals

Our chemical is a corrosion inhibitor,which is suited for removal of scales from Cooling Towers and wide range of cooling systems.

Epoxy flooring

PP FRP pipeline supply and work

PP FRP tank supply
